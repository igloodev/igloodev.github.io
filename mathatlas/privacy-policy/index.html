Privacy Policy

MathAtlas ("we", "our", or "us") is built by igloodev. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo URL from the authentication provider. We store this in Firebase Authentication solely to identify your account.

Usage Data

We store your bookmarked formulas in Firestore, linked to your user ID. We do not collect analytics, crash reports, or behavioural tracking beyond what Firebase Authentication requires to function.

Device Information

Firebase may collect basic device metadata (OS version, app version) as part of its standard operation. We do not access this data directly.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • To sync your bookmarks across devices.
  • To determine your subscription status (Free or Pro).
  • To respond to support requests sent to our support email.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is stored in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore and Firebase Authentication), governed by Google's privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

Your account data is retained for as long as your account exists. You may request deletion at any time by contacting us. We will delete your Firestore data and Firebase Auth record within 30 days of a verified request.

5. Children's Privacy

MathAtlas is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Security

All data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Data at rest is protected by Firebase's security rules, which restrict access to authenticated owners only.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. The "last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance.
